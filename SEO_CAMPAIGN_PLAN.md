## ⚠️ Important Notes on Backlinks

### Realistic Expectations:
- **40,000 dofollow backlinks in 6 months is unrealistic and potentially harmful**
- Quality > Quantity (100 high-quality backlinks > 10,000 spammy ones)
- Google penalizes unnatural link building
- Focus on 1,000-2,000 HIGH-QUALITY backlinks in 6 months

### Safe Link Building Rules:
1. ✅ Natural link velocity (gradual growth)
2. ✅ Diverse anchor text (not all exact match)
3. ✅ Relevant, high-authority sources
4. ✅ Editorial, earned links (not purchased)
5. ❌ Avoid link farms, PBNs, bulk directory submissions
6. ❌ No automated link building tools
7. ❌ No buying links in bulk
